Depression facts

  • Depression is a common mental disorder, more than 300 million people of all ages suffer from depression.
  • It is the leading cause of disability.
  • Depression can lead to suicide.
  • There are effective treatments for depression.

Female vs Male depression

Female vs Male depression in 2014

Depression rates vary by gender, age, and ethnicity. In general, women are at higher risk of depression than men, ethnic minorities are at greater risk of depression than non-Hispanic whites, and adults aged 45-64 are more likely than any other age group to have a diagnosis of depression.

Male/female facts

  • Women are more likely to ruminate when feeling depressed.
  • Men with depression are more likely to abuse alcohol and other substances.
  • Men's symptoms of depression may be harder for others to recognize.
  • Women are more likely than men to have depression and a co-existing eating disorder.
  • Men are more likely to commit suicide.
